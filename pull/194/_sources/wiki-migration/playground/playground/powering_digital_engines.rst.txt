Introduction
============

Sitting between the signal chain front- and back-ends are the data processing,
communication, and human-interface blocks. These "digital engines" are a
critical part of any system and require as much attention to the power system
for them as the rest of the signal chain. Below you will find reference designs,
recommendations, and guidance for powering these digital engines.

FPGAs
=====

A Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) offers a high degree of flexibility when
it comes to building a digital processing system. However, this flexibility can
make the job of powering the FPGA more challenging. The material at the links
below will help you, the designer, create a better power system in less time.
