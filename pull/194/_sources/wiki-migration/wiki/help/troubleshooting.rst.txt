Troubleshooting Wiki Issues
===========================

Make sure you are logged in
---------------------------

Goto https://wiki.analog.com/?do=check have a look at the new text at the top that says if you are logged in or not.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/wiki/help/do_check.png
   :width: 800

If it:

-  says "You are currently not logged in"

   -  scroll to the top of the screen, and click the "login" button at the top
      right.

If you are an Analog Employee - and want the privileges of an Analog employee in
the wiki, your my analog account must be tied to your analog.com email address.
It should then say you are part of the group "analog_employees". If it doesn't
say this - then you didn't use your analog.com email address to validate your
myanalog account with.
