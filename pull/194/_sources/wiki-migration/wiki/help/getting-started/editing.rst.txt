Login
-----

Anyone can view content on the Wiki. If you'd like to edit content, however, you must register on the site and be logged in.

To login, click the login link at the top of the page. If you already have an account with EngineerZone or myAnalog, use that account to login here. Otherwise, click the registration tab and sign up for an account. Once you have confirmed your email address and chosen a username, you will be returned to the Wiki.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/wiki/help/getting-started/wiki-login.png
   :width: 500px
   :height: 84px

Wiki Editor
-----------

Once you are logged in, click "Edit this page" at top right.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/wiki/help/getting-started/wiki-edit-link.png
   :width: 600px
   :height: 175px

Clicking on this link will bring up the Wiki Editor where you can make changes. The toolbar offered at the top of the editor provides quick access to most of the basic Wiki syntax. If you need help with the syntax, you’ll find information in the :doc:`help </wiki-migration/wiki/help>` section including a quick reference pdf that you can download.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/wiki/help/getting-started/wiki-toolbar.png
   :width: 700px
   :height: 110px

Previewing and Publishing Content
---------------------------------

-  After making changes to the content, click the Preview button to view how changes will appear on the Wiki. The preview will appear below the buttons.
-  Provide a brief description of your changes in the Edit Summary field.
-  Click Save and your content will be published to the Wiki.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/wiki/help/getting-started/wiki-steps.png

.. tip::

   You will need to add text to the Edit Summary field in order to save your content.


Your content will be published to the site as a draft version and will be reviewed by an Analog Devices employee. Once your edit has been approved it will appear as an approved page on the Wiki.
