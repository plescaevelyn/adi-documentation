System Demonstration Platform
=============================

The SDP Family consists of:

-  Controller Boards

   -  :doc:`SDP-B </wiki-migration/resources/eval/sdp/sdp-b>` - provides information on the SDP Blackfin
   -  :doc:`SDP-S </wiki-migration/resources/eval/sdp/sdp-s/getting_started>` - provides information on the SDP Serial
   -  :doc:`SDP-H1 </wiki-migration/resources/eval/sdp/sdp-h1>` - provides information on the SDP High Speed

-  Interposer Boards

   -  :doc:`Breakout Board </wiki-migration/resources/eval/sdp/breakout_board>` - provides information on the SDP Breakout board.

-  3rd Party Tools

   -  :doc:`BeMicro SDK/SDP Interposer </wiki-migration/resources/eval/sdp/bemicro_sdp>` - adapter that connects SDP daughter boards to the BeMicro SDK.
   -  :doc:`SDP-I-FMC Interposer </wiki-migration/resources/eval/sdp/sdp-fmc>` - adapter that connects SDP daughter boards to Xilinx FPGA Evaluation kits with 3.3VIO
   -  :doc:`PMOD to SDP Interposer </wiki-migration/resources/eval/sdp/sdp-pmod>` - adapter that connects ADI PMOD boards to SDP-B evaluation controller board
