Bluetooth Smart Device Apps
===========================

The ADICUP3029 comes complete with on board Bluetooth 5.0 low energy. In order
to use the ADICUP3029 along with various different add on boards, Analog Devices
(ADI) has created a smart device app that will work with iOS devices.
