EV-MOTE-AGILE-900Z
==================

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/eval/user-guides/ev-mote-agile-900z/skit_flyer.png

Description
-----------

The EV-MOTE-AGILE-900Z is the secondary kit to demo the AgileNet-6T networking
solution. This kit only provides additional nodes and is a supplementary kit to
the EV-KIT-AGILE-900Z (Primary kit). This evaluation kit contains the following
hardware and equipment:

-  10 EV-MTE-AGILE-900Z evaluation boards.
-  10 antenna pigtails.
-  10 antennas.
-  10 Li batteries, 3.6 V AA size.
