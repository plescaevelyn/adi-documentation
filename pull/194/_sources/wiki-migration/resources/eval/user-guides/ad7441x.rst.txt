AD74412R/AD74413R Wiki
======================

Introduction
------------

The AD74412R and AD74413R are quad-channel software configurable input/output
devices. They are designed for, but not exclusive to, building control & process
control applications. The products provide analog output, analog input, digital
input, resistance temperature detector (RTD) and thermocouple measurements
integrated into a single chip solution with a serial peripheral interface (SPI).
Datasheet and evaluation details can be found at the relevant product pages. See
the links below:

-  :adi:`AD74412R product page <en/products/ad74412r.html>`
-  :adi:`AD74413R product page <en/products/ad74413r.html>`
