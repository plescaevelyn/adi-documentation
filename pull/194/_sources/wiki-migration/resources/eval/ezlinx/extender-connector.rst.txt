=========================================

The Hirose FX8 120P-SV(91) extender connector is used for daughter board connections. This allows for additional functionality to be added to the *ez*\ LINX hardware. Figure 1 below shows the circuit implementation of the extender connector J23 and J26. Connector J26 is a three pin header connector and allows for the CAN1 signals of the ADSP-BF548 to be routed to an external daughter board.
