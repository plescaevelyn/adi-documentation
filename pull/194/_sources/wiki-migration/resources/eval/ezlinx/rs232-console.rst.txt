ezLINXâ„˘ RS-232 Console Implementation
=====================================

The RS-232 console connector is used for accessing the console of the uCLinux
kernel running on the ADSP-BF548 processor. It uses the ADM3202 RS-232 line
driver and receiver to connect to UART1 of the ADSP-BF548. The RS-232 signals
connect to a DB-9 connector, J4. A circuit implementation of the RS-232 console
is shown below.
