Hardware Implementation
-----------------------

The Isolated USB port is implemented using the :adi:`adum3160` Full speed USB Isolator. The :adi:`adum3160` connects to the integrated USB PHY of the ADSP-BF548 and is capable of functioning at data rates of up to 12Mbit/s. Figure 1 below shows a circuit diagram of the implementation of the :adi:`adum3160` on the *ez*\ LINX hardware.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/ezlinx/isolatedusb.png
   :alt: Figure 1.
   :align: center
   :width: 900

The VBUS1 and VDD1 of the :adi:`adum3160` is powered from the 5V bus power supply. VBUS2 and VDD2 is powered from 3.3V generated by the *ez*\ LINX power supply. A mini USB-B connector is used for easy connection to a USB master.
