Prototyping Platform
====================

This article will provide an overview of the Analog Devices Prototyping
Platform, which itself is a collection of tools, integrations, examples, and
hardware to accelerate design flows. The platform itself is architected to
leverage and connect common and standardized tools, including filling in gaps
where necessary, to help shorten development cycles. Different solutions may be
available for different application spaces since this platform tries to tailor
or curate options based around what users want and not what is convenient.
