CMOS/LVDS Pinout
================

The CMOS/LVDS connection on :doc:`DPG2 </wiki-migration/resources/eval/dpg/dpg2>` and :doc:`DPG3 </wiki-migration/resources/eval/dpg/dpg3>` uses two AMP/Tyco 1469169-1 connectors, placed side-by-side, with 139.2mil spacing between the centers of the innermost pins on both connectors. The mating connector on the evaluation board side is two AMP/Tyco 1469028-1. Note that both connectors are always required.
