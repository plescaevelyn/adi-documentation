Example 2
---------

The following example uses Simulation Stimuli, Simulation Probe, T Connection
and Schematic Terminal to produce the frequency response of General (1st-Order),
General (2nd-Order) , and Deemphasis filters set at their default values. (Note
that you have to press the Stimuli button to see the graphs.)
