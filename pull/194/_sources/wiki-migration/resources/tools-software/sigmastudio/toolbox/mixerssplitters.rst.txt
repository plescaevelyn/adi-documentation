Mixers/Splitters
================

:doc:`Click here to return to the Toolbox page </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox>`

The Mixers/Splitters library of the ToolBox gives access to blocks that let you
combine or split signals in various ways. The following block's are available:

-  :doc:`Stereo Mixer </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/mixerssplitters/stereomixer>`
-  :doc:`Nx1 Mixer Linear(2x1) </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/mixerssplitters/nx1mixerlinear>`
-  :doc:`NxM Mixer </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/mixerssplitters/nxmmixer>`
-  :doc:`Cross Mixer (2 Inputs) </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/mixerssplitters/crossmixer2inputs>`
-  :doc:`Cross Mixer (3 Inputs) </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/mixerssplitters/crossmixer3inputs>`
-  :doc:`Cross Mixer (8 Inputs) </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/mixerssplitters/crossmixer8inputs>`
