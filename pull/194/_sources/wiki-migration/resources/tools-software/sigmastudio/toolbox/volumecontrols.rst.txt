The Volume Controls category of the ToolBox gives you access to blocks that
control signal levels: mute, gain sliders, and envelopes.

Following is a list of the available blocks:

Volume Controls
===============

-  :doc:`Single Volume </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/singlevolume>`; This is the most commonly used block for a simple volume control. The slewing version is desired for most applications.
-  :doc:`Single Volume Shared </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/singlevolumeshared>`
-  :doc:`Growable Single Volume Control </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/growablesinglevolumecontrol>`

-  :doc:`Single Slew Ext Vol </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/singleslewextvol>`
-  :doc:`Single SW Slew Vol (Adjustable) </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/singleswslewvoladjustable>`
-  :doc:`Adjustable Slew Control </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/adjustableslewcontrol>`

-  :doc:`Multiple Volume Control </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/multiplevolumecontrol>`
-  :doc:`Multiple Volume Shared </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/volumecontrols/multiplevolumeshared>`
