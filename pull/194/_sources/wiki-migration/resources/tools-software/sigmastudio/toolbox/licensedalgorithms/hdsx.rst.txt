HDSX Surround Decoder; HDSX Virtual Center; HDSX Stereo Sound Widening 360
==========================================================================

:doc:`Click here to return to the Licensed Algorithms page </wiki-migration/resources/tools-software/sigmastudio/toolbox/licensedalgorithms>`

.. important::

   These blocks are not included in the SigmaStudio installation. Please contact the company Kronoton directly for licensing and test information under `info@hdsx.audio <https://wiki.analog.com/info@hdsx.audio>`_.


.. important::

   Decoders, running on DSPs with at least four channel input, can also be delivered as automotive versions with 2x2.0 input (Front/Rear)


HDSX Surround 2.0 - 5.0/5.1 RealCenter®
---------------------------------------

**Available on ADAU 1452 and others on request**

RealCenter technology for precise center extraction and localization. TruDirection® Surround channels for enhanced directional localization. Decoded output channels: L,R,C,(LFE),Sl,Sr.

HDSX Surround 2.0 - 7.0/7.1 RealCenter
--------------------------------------

**Available on ADAU 1452 and others on request**

RealCenter technology for precise center extraction and localization. TruDirection® Surround channels for enhanced directional localization. Decoded output channels: L,R,C,(LFE),Sl,Sr,BSl,BSr.

HDSX Surround 2.0 - 5.0/5.1 LowMips
-----------------------------------

**Available on ADAU 1452, ADAU 144x, ADAU 1701 and others on request**

L+R Center Channel. TruDirection® Surround channels for enhanced directional localization. Decoded output channels: L,R,C,(LFE),Sl,Sr.

HDSX Virtual Center \| Speech enhancer
--------------------------------------

**Available on ADAU 1452 and others on request**

This block enables independent volume control of the phantom center within stereo sources. It is not a hidden L+R upmix. A crossover frequency can be set to split the phantom center.

**Available on ADAU 1452 and others on request**

HDSX Stereo Sound Widening 360
------------------------------

**Available on SigmaDSP 100,200,300**

HDSX® - High Definition Sound Expansion increases the sound field, especially of little- and small-field positioned sound sources by expanding the sound field and the spatial hearing experience in a very unique way. It creates a new spatial quality of small speaker entertainment like used in mobile devices, flatscreen TV's, gaming- and automotive solutions. Used in headphones, the HDSX stereo spatializer creates a 360 sound field without inhead effects.

More infos under www.hdsx.audio.
