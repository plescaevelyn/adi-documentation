Setup Details
=============

As shown in the image below, the setup includes the Raspberry Pi 4 board,
Daughter Card, AD2428MINI board (Master), EVAL-AD2428WC1BZ (Subnode - 0), and
EVAL-AD2428WB1BZ (Subnode - 1), with the Master and Subnodes connected through a
UTP cable.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/tools-software/a2bv2/a2braspberrypi/setup_image.png
   :align: center

I2S signals are generated by the Raspberry Pi board using ALSA commands and
transmitted to the AD2428MINI board. A signal analyzer can be employed to probe
the GPIO pins on the board.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/tools-software/a2bv2/a2braspberrypi/setup_picture.png
   :align: center
