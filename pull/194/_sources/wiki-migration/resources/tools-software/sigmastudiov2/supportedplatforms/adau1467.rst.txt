ADAU1467
========

The ADAU1463/ADAU1467 are automotive qualified audio processors that far exceed
the digital signal processing capabilities of earlier SigmaDSP® devices. They
are pin and register compatible with each other, as well as with the
ADAU1450/ADAU1451/ADAU1452 SigmaDSP® processors.

More details - :adi:`ADAU1467 <en/products/adau1467.html>`
