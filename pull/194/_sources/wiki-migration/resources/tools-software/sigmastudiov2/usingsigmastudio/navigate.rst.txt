Navigate through a project
==========================

This section provides different ways of accessing canvas and settings windows in
a project.

--------------

Following are the different ways of accessing canvas and settings windows in a
project:

1. Users can double click on the platform to open its canvas or right click on
   the platform and open its settings and canvas (refer the image below).

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/tools-software/sigmastudiov2/usingsigmastudio/contextmenu.png
   :width: 400

2. Users can open the processor's settings and the canvas from the project
   window (refer the image below).

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/tools-software/sigmastudiov2/usingsigmastudio/projectwindow.png
   :width: 400

3. Users can open the processor's settings and the canvas from the schematic
   window using the settings and canvas icon (refer the image below).

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/tools-software/sigmastudiov2/usingsigmastudio/schematic.png
   :width: 400
