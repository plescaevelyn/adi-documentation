Load/Save Shape
===============

A shape in the SigmaStudio+ application can be saved from the 'Save shape contents' option to .shape file, which can then be loaded to another shape or a custom platform through the 'Load Shape Contents' option, by this a '.shape' file can be imported to a custom platform. This is to bring re-usability of algorithms seamlessly into the system.
