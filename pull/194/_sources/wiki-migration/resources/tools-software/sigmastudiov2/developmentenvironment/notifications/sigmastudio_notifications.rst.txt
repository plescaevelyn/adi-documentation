Notifications
=============

Notifications are displayed on the right side of the screen on which
SigmaStudio+ application is placed. Positive and negative notifications (for
errors) are displayed using different colours. Notifications shall disappear
after the predefined time period. The notification service supports upto 7
messages at a time. If there are more messages, they are queued and displayed
when earlier messages disappear.
