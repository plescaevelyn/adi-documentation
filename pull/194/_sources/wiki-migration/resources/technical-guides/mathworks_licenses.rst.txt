Overview
========

The following are directions to procure the necessary trial licenses for a
model. If you have access to MATLAB, but are not sure if you have all of these
licenses, you can type “ver” from the MATLAB command line to see what toolboxes
are installed on your local host. If you do not have access to all of the
required licenses, please follow the procedure below to request the licenses
that you require.

Existing MathWorks Customer
===========================

If you have access to a license of MATLAB that is current on maintenance from
MathWorks, you may automatically request trial licenses from MathWorks.com. (if
you do not have a supported version of MATLAB, skip to Step 2) Procedure to
request trials from MathWorks.com:

1. Click Get Trial Software from MathWorks.com

2. Enter your MathWorks.com account information into the Free Product Trial
   page:

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/technical-guides/mathworks/licenses/1_account_info.png

3. In the Free Product Trial page, select the Custom Trial offer:

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/technical-guides/mathworks/licenses/2_custom_trial.png

4. Select R2016a and the OS you are using:

|image1| |image2|

5. Select the products that you do not have access to from the list on Page One
   of this guide. Note, even if you have access to some of these licenses via a
   network server, for example MATLAB, you may want to request trials for these
   as well to guarantee access while you evaluate the model.

.. image:: https://wiki.analog.com/_media/resources/technical-guides/mathworks/licenses/5_select_toolboxes.png

6. The trial license will be available be sent to you via email immediately and
   available from your MathWorks.com account.

New MathWorks Customer
======================

If you are new to MathWorks, and do not have access to a current (on
maintenance) license of MATLAB, please follow these steps:

1. Register for a MathWorks.com account. This will be required for any trial
   license of MathWorks software.

2. Once you have a registered MathWorks.com account, send an email to the
   following MathWorks contacts to request your trial:

-  Corey Mathis – corey.mathis@mathworks.com
-  Noam Levine – noam.levine@mathworks.com
-  Please include “[Model Name] model” in the Subject Line
