Operational Amplifier Experimenter Board
========================================

Description
-----------

This board implements many of the basic op-amp configurations including several inverting and noininverting gains, differentiator, integrator, and difference amplifier. LTspice and design files are available here:

:git-education_tools:`Op Amp Experimenter Design Files and LTspice Simulation <experiment-boards/op_amp_experimenter>`
