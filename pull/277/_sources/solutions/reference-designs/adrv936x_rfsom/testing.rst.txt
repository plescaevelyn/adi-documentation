ADRV936x RF SOM Certifications
==============================

Information regarding environmental and mechanical testing is listed here. Sinusoidal vibration, random vibration, shock testing etc. The ADRVx RF SOM family is tested against various military (https://mil-std.org) and Information Technology Standards (NIST) using external vendors with the appropriate equipment. All details and results of such are maintained in the test reports below.

ADRV9364-Z7020
--------------

   -  This report contains tests results for:

      -  EN 55022:2010 unwanted emissions
      -  EN 55024:2010 immunity

-  `SOM1 Environmental Test Report <resources/1604-049na_analog_devices_inc_report.pdf>`_

   -  This report contains the results for:

      -  MIL-STD-202G testing
      -  MIL-STD-810G testing

-  `UL 94V0 flammability rating <https://www.ulttc.com/en/solutions/test-methods/combustion-fire/flammability/flammability-ul-94-v.html>`_

   -  `UL On line Certification <resources/e229342_sunshine_zpmv2.pdf>`_
