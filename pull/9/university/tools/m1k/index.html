ADALM1000

The ADALM1000 Active Learning Module is an easy to use tool available from Analog Devices Inc. that can be used to introduce fundamentals of electrical engineering in a self or instructor led setting across different experience levels and backgrounds.

With the ADALM1000, students will have at their finger tips two high-precision analog channels, each capable of generating or measuring analog signals at up to 100 kilosamples per second (kSPS). Combined with the cross platform (OS X, Windows, and Linux) Pixelpulse 2 graphical interface, continuous streaming of voltage and current measurements into a simple GUI enable a variety of explorations across impedance, time and frequency domains.

Designed to fit in a shirt pocket or backpack and entirely USB-powered, the ADALM1000 provides a fully portable and self-contained hardware module.
