    ADALM1000

    The ADALM1000 (M1K) Active Learning Module is an easy to use tool available from Analog Devices Inc. that can be used to introduce fundamentals of electrical engineering in a self or instructor led setting across different experience levels and backgrounds.

    With the ADALM1000, students will have at their finger tips two high-precision analog channels, each capable of measuring or sourcing voltage and current signals at up to 100 kilosamples per second (kSPS).
