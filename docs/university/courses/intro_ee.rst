Introduction to Electrical Engineering
===============================================================================

The course aims to help early-stage electrical and computer engineering students
develop broad analysis skill set that enable engineered solutions addressing a
broad array of societal needs. The materials emphasize experiment-centric
learning combining paper-and-pencil analysis, simulation, and practical
experimentation across analog/digital circuits, electronics, and programming.

.. note::

   The textbooks and teaching materials provided here are considered open source
   and available for free use in non-commercial educational and academic settings.
   The only requirement is that they continue to retain the attribution to
   Analog Devices Inc.
