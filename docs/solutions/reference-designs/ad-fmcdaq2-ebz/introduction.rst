Introduction
============

The :adi:`AD-FMCDAQ2-EBZ <eval-ad-fmcdaq2-ebz>`
module is comprised of the :adi:`AD9680 <ad9680>`
dual, 14-bit, 1.0 GSPS, JESD204B ADC, the :adi:`AD9144 <ad9144>` quad,
16-bit, 2.8 GSPS, JESD204B DAC, the :adi:`AD9523-1 <ad9523-1>`
clock, and power management components. It is clocked by an internally
generated carrier platform via the FMC connector, comprising a completely self
contained data acquisition and signal synthesis prototyping platform. In an
FMC footprint (84 mm × 69 mm), the module’s combination of wideband data
conversion, clocking, and power closely approximates real-world hardware and
software for system prototyping and design, with no compromise in signal chain
performance. For additional information or to purchase the
:adi:`AD-FMCDAQ2-EBZ`, please visit the product page.

Features
--------

-  Includes schematics, layout, BOM, Gerber files, HDL, Linux® drivers, IIO
   Oscilloscope, VisualANALOG,...
-  FMC-compatible form factor
-  Powered from single FMC connector
-  Provides two channels of ADC and two channels of DAC with full
   synchronization capabilities

AD9680-1000 ADC
---------------

-  JESD204B (subclass 1) 4-lane coded serial digital outputs
-  SFDR: 80 dBc at 1 GHz AIN, 1.0 GSPS
-  Noise density: –154 dBFS/Hz @ 1.0 GSPS
-  2 GHz of usable analog input full power bandwidth
-  Two integrated DDCs per channel

AD9144 DAC
----------

-  JESD204B (subclass 1) coded serial digital outputs
-  Supports complex signal bandwidths up to 800 MHz
-  6-carrier GSM IMD = 75 dBc at 75 MHz IF
-  SFDR = 85 dBc (BW = 300 MHz) at DC IF
-  Selectable 2×, 4×, 8× interpolation filters
