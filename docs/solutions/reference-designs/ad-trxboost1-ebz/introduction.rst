Introduction
============

The AD-TRXBOOST1-EBZ module is comprised of the ADL5610 which is an 18dB RF
fixed gain block that can operate between 30MHz and 6 GHz with a low noise
figure of 2.2dB, the ADL5521 which is a 20dB LNA that can operate between 400MHz
and 4GHz with a low noise figure of 0.8dB, along with the ADP2370 switch
regulator and two ADP7104 LDOs.

For additional information or to purchase the AD-TRXBOOST1-EBZ, visit:
www.analog.com/AD-TRXBOOST1-EBZ

Features
--------

-  Includes schematics, layout, BOM, Gerber files
-  Powered from single Keyed connector that matches the header on the FMCOMMS2/3/4 series.
-  Provides one channel TX and one channel RX boost.

ADL5610 Gain Block
------------------

-  30MHz to 6 GHz
-  Fixed gain of 18.4dB
-  Noise figure: 2.2dB at 900MHz
-  OIP3: 38.8dBm at 900MHz
-  P1dB: 20.4dBm at 900MHz
-  Single 5V supply

ADL5521 LNA
-----------

-  400MHz to 4GHz
-  Fixed gain of 20.8dB at 900MHz
-  Noise figure: 0.8dB at 900MHz
-  OIP3: 37.0dBm at 900MHz
-  P1dB: 21.8dBm at 900MHz
-  Single supply between 3V and 5V
