ADALM1000
=========

.. image:: images/adalm1000_angle_small.png
   :align: left
   :width: 200px

The :adi:`ADALM1000` (M1K) Active Learning Module is an easy to use tool
available from Analog Devices Inc. that can be used to introduce fundamentals
of electrical engineering in a self or instructor led setting across different
experience levels and backgrounds.

With the ADALM1000, students have at their fingertips two high-precision
analog channels, each capable of sourcing or measuring voltage and current
signals at up to 100 kilosamples per second (kSPS). Combined with the
cross-platform Pixelpulse 2 graphical interface, continuous streaming of
voltage and current measurements enables a variety of explorations across
impedance, time and frequency domains.

.. figure:: images/adalm1000_block.png
   :align: center
   :width: 500px

   ADALM1000 Block Diagram

Designed to fit in a shirt pocket or backpack and entirely USB-powered, the
ADALM1000 provides a fully portable and self-contained hardware module.
